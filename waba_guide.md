Meta WhatsApp Business API (WABA) Onboarding & Compliance Guide

This document serves as a master checklist for onboarding a new WABA number. Meta (formerly Facebook) enforces strict policies regarding business identity, website structure, and messaging behavior.

1. Website Verification Requirements

Your website is the primary evidence Meta uses to verify your business. If any of these are missing, your Business Verification or Display Name will be rejected.

A. Legal & Identity Alignment

Legal Business Name: Your registered company name (e.g., Acme Technologies Pvt Ltd) must be visible in the website footer or the "About Us" page. It must match your Tax/Incorporation documents.

Display Name Evidence: The name you want to show on WhatsApp (e.g., Acme Support) must be clearly branded on the website (Logo, Header, or Text).

Physical Address: The address on your website's "Contact Us" page should match the address in your Meta Business Portfolio.

B. Technical Standards

HTTPS (SSL Certificate): The site must be secure. http:// sites are automatically rejected.

Active Status: The website must be fully functional. "Coming Soon" or "Under Construction" pages lead to immediate rejection.

Domain Verification: You must prove ownership of the domain via Meta-Tag, HTML file upload, or DNS TXT record within the Meta Business Suite.

C. Mandatory Policy Pages

Meta's 2026 guidelines require explicit data transparency:

Privacy Policy: Must be a dedicated page explaining how you handle user data (especially data collected via WhatsApp).

Terms of Service (ToS): Outlining the rules of engagement for your services.

Contact Info: A clear email address or phone number for customer support.

2. Phone Number Requirements

Before registering a number for the API, ensure it meets these criteria:

Fresh Status: The number cannot be currently active on the standard WhatsApp or WhatsApp Business mobile apps.

Deletion Process: If the number is currently in use on an app, you must go to Settings > Account > Delete my account before starting the API registration.

Verification Capability: The number must be able to receive an international SMS or a Voice Call to receive the 6-digit OTP.

4. Documentation for Business Verification

To move from "Unverified" to "Verified" status, you need:

Proof of Legal Entity: Certificate of Incorporation, GST/VAT Certificate, or Business License.

Proof of Business Address: Utility Bill (Electricity/Phone), Bank Statement, or Tax Registration.

Note: Documents must be in a Meta-supported language or have a certified translation.

5. Prohibited & Restricted Categories

If your website contains content related to these categories, your WABA will be rejected or banned:

Prohibited: Adult content, Tobacco, Alcohol (in some regions), Gambling, Weapons, or Illegal Drugs.

Restricted: Pharmacy, Supplements, or Financial Services (require additional licensing proof).

AI Policy (2026): General-purpose AI chatbots (unrestricted "talk to me" bots) are prohibited. Bots must be task-specific (e.g., "Check Order Status").

# Meta Policy for WhatsApp Business Account (WABA) Display Names

The Meta policy for WhatsApp Business Account (WABA) display names ensures that customers can easily identify the business they are communicating with. Meta enforces these rules during the Business Verification and On-Behalf-Of (OBO) onboarding processes.

---

## 1. Compliance & Branding

Your display name must be a **faithful representation** of your business. It cannot be used to mislead users or misrepresent your brand.

* **Relationship:** The name must have a clear relationship with your business (e.g., it should match the name on your website or official documents).
* **No Generic Names:** You cannot use generic terms like "Shoes" or "London." It must be "John’s Shoes" or "London Coffee House."
* **No Geographic Locations Alone:** You cannot name your account "New York" unless it is part of the brand name (e.g., "New York Pizza").
* **External Branding:** The name must match the branding on your website, social media, or legal documents.

---

## 2. Formatting Rules

Meta is strict about how the name is written to maintain a professional appearance.

| Rule Type | Requirement | Example (Incorrect) | Example (Correct) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Capitalization** | Cannot be all caps (except acronyms). Must use title case. | BEST BAKERY | Best Bakery |
| **Spacing** | Must have proper spacing. | BestBakery | Best Bakery |
| **Punctuation** | No unnecessary punctuation or emojis. | Best Bakery!!! 😊 | Best Bakery |
| **Extra Words** | Do not add "Official" or "WhatsApp" to the name. | Best Bakery Official | Best Bakery |
| **Length** | Recommended under 25 characters for display. | — | — |

---

## 3. URL & Industry Restrictions

* **No URLs:** You cannot include a website address (e.g., `www.bestbakery.com`) as the display name.
* **Prohibited Industries:** Names cannot reference illegal products, tobacco, weapons, alcohol (in certain regions), or adult content, as per the [WhatsApp Commerce Policy](https://www.whatsapp.com/policies/commerce-policy/).

---

## 4. Sub-Brands and Departments

If you have multiple departments, you can include them in the name, provided the main brand is present.

* **Acceptable:** `Best Bakery - Customer Support` or `Best Bakery [Deliveries]`
* **Unacceptable:** `Customer Support` (too generic).

---

## 5. Verification and Changes

* **Review Process:** Once you submit a name, Meta’s automated systems (and sometimes manual reviewers) will check it. If it’s rejected, you will see a "Display Name Disapproved" status in your WhatsApp Manager.
* **Editing:** You can change your display name in the **WhatsApp Manager** under **Phone Numbers**. However, once your account is "Official" (Green Tick), changing the name requires a re-verification process.

> **Pro-Tip:** If your business is not yet verified, your display name will only appear to users after you pass the Business Verification process or reach a specific messaging tier.
